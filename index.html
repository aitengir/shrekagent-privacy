Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: July 1, 2025

📱 Information We Collect

  • Voice data: Recorded temporarily to process your commands. Not stored permanently.
  • Location data: Used only to provide weather information. Not stored or shared.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • Voice commands are sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription
  • Text is processed by Anthropic Claude AI to generate responses
  • Responses are converted to speech using ElevenLabs
  • Location is used only for weather queries via OpenWeatherMap

🤝 Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI Whisper — voice transcription
  • Anthropic Claude — AI responses
  • ElevenLabs — text to speech
  • OpenWeatherMap — weather data

🔒 Data Storage & Sharing

Shrek Agent does not permanently store your voice recordings, location data, or personal information on our servers. All processing is done in real-time. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

👶 Children's Privacy

Shrek Agent is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
