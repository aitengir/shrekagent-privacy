🧅 Shrek Agent — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Shrek Agent collects the following information to provide its services:

  • Voice data: Recorded temporarily to process your commands. Not stored permanently.
  • Location data: Used only to provide weather information. Not stored or shared.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Voice commands are sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription
  • Text is processed by Anthropic Claude AI to generate responses
  • Responses are converted to speech using ElevenLabs
  • Location is used only for weather queries via OpenWeatherMap

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI Whisper — voice transcription
  • Anthropic Claude — AI responses
  • ElevenLabs — text to speech
  • OpenWeatherMap — weather data

4. Data Storage

Shrek Agent does not permanently store your voice recordings, location data, or personal information on our servers. All processing is done in real-time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to provide the service (third-party APIs listed above).

6. Children's Privacy

Shrek Agent is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
